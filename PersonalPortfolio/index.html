Hi, I am Priyanka Garg - Full Stack Software developer. Previous to software developer, I was a substitute teacher where i was helping middle and Elementry School kids in math and science. My journey into software development became directed & focused when I joined an in-person, full-time, and full-stack software development bootcamp at the Nashville Software School. Alongside learning hard skills-my cohort focused on modern web development with ASP.NET as a back-end solution.

In my free time, I like to learn new tricks (food,Game) that keep my brain and body healthy and active. I am good at finding the things that should be fixed. Let me learn and work with you, and see what we can fix along the way. Currently, I'm looking for a junior role, and I really want to be somewhere that I can grow quickly as a developer. So naturally, Im looking for a place that has a solid mentorship culture and will push my limits daily. If you'd like to get together to talk code over a cup of coffee or a beer,you can find my contact info in the footer.
